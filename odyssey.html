Welcome to Odyssey!

Odyssey is a webring created by FrenchGuy and Scovinq

What is a webring?

A webring (or web ring) is a collection of websites linked together in a circular structure, and usually organized around a specific theme, often educational or social. They were popular in the 1990s and early 2000s, particularly among amateur websites. -Wikipedia

Rules

    1. Websites must be kid friendly!
    2. Websites must not contain viruses
    3. Websites must contain the webring's widget

Joining

To join, please create an "add to webring" ticket on Discord with a similar message to the following:

Hello! I am the owner of [website link] and I would like to join Odyssey.
